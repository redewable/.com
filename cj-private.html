What "Net Exit Equity" Means

Your 15% is calculated on net proceeds after: (1) return of capital to all equity holders, (2) repayment of any project-level debt, and (3) transaction costs. On a $40M exit with minimal debt, net proceeds could be $38M+, making your 15% worth $5.7M+.

Token Allocation

The $RDW token is the governance and utility token for the ReDewable protocol. Your 2% allocation vests over 24 months with a 6-month cliff. This is separate from your equity ‚Äî additional upside beyond this single project if the protocol scales.

Treasury Council Seat

As a seed investor, you receive one of five seats on the Foundation board. You will vote on treasury allocations >$50K, approve major protocol decisions, have visibility into all fund flows, and hold fiduciary duty to the Foundation.
